Oyster Stew

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of bacon
  • Half gallon of heavy cream
  • milk(to fill stockpot)
  • 5 medium Russet Potatoes
  • 1 onion; diced
  • 3 cans of oysters(in water)
  • 1 garlic clove; diced

Directions

  1. Saute bacon, onions, and garlic until bacon is crisp and onions are carmelized
  2. Peel and dice potatoes. Add to stockpot with cream and milk.
  3. Heat med-high to boiling
  4. Add the bacon, onions and garlic.
  5. Stir occasionally
  6. Simmer until potatoes are cooked thoroughly
  7. Add oysters
  8. Enjoy!
